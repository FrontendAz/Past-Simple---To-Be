📚 Past Simple - To Be

Past Simple "To Be" keçmişdə baş vermiş vəziyyətləri və hadisələri ifadə etmək üçün istifadə olunur.

📌 Formula

Positive Subject + was / were
Negative Subject + was not (wasn't) / were not (weren't)
Question Was/Were + Subject ?

👨‍🏫 Was / Were Cədvəli

Subject To Be
I was
He was
She was
It was
You were
We were
They were

✅ Positive Sentences

  • I was happy.
  • She was at school.
  • They were tired.
  • We were friends.

❌ Negative Sentences

  • I wasn't busy.
  • He wasn't at home.
  • They weren't late.
  • We weren't hungry.

❓ Questions

  • Was she happy?
  • Were they at school?
  • Was he tired?
  • Were you busy?

💬 Short Answers

Question Answer
Was he at home? Yes, he was.
Was she sad? No, she wasn't.
Were they friends? Yes, they were.
Were you busy? No, we weren't.

🕒 Time Expressions

Yesterday Last week Last year Last month Last night Two days ago In 2019

📖 İstifadəsi

⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ I were happy.
✅ I was happy.

❌ He were at school.
✅ He was at school.
